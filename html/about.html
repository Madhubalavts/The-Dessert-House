About Us

Food of The Gods, Freshly Baked!

Since 2024, we've been serving our guests the best quality treats, traditionally made and presented with care.

AUTHENTIC RECIPES

Our products are based on traditional home-style recipes using fresh ingredients.

BAKED WITH LOVE

Our passion for baking is poured into every recipe, serving smiles on a plate everyday.

COMMITTED TO QUALITY

From our ingredients to our kitchen operations and guest services, we always prioritize quality.

HONESTLY PRICED

We constantly strive to offer the best products at the right prices.

"The dessert house" would be a dedicated establishment for sweet foods and indulgent range of
offerings that include brownies, cakes, desserts, chocolates, breads, and savouries.

From our humble beginnings in 2024, when the first The dessert house pastry store opened its doors
at the place of Karaikudi, Tamil Nadu, India, we've grown to become a Pan-India chain of patisseries
with stores in over 10 cities.

Our mission is to spread happiness by serving smiles on a plate, and this journey continues as we open
The dessert house patisseries across the country.
