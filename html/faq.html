Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Who is the owner of The Dessert House?

+
Are the brownies eggless?
Yes, we offer eggless variants for most of our cakes, pastries, and brownies.
+
How can I order?
You can:
  • Order directly from our website. www.thedesserthouse.com and click Order Online

How long do the brownies last?
Our brownies stay fresh for 4 days if stored in a cool, dry place at ambient temperature.
+
How should I store your products?
a.All products are baked/prepared fresh daily without presevatives.
b.Shelf life is mentioned on product descriptions or labels(for packaged goods like breads, cookies, crackers).
+
Can I microwave or heat the products?
a.Breads: Should not be microwaved.
b.Savouries: Can be grilled or oven-heated.
c.Cakes and Brownes: Best enjoyed at room temperature.
d.If refrigerated, remove 15-20 minutes before eating.
+
Do you offer sugar-free, vegan, or gluten-free products?
Not on our regular menu.
+
Does your website also deliver on Sundays and public holidays?
The Dessert House works on all sundays and holidays except under uncontrollable situations like bad weather or civil disorder like bandhs.
